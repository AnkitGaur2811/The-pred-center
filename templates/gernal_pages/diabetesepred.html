Diabetese Prediction

Diabetes is a metabolic disorder characterized by high blood sugar levels over a prolonged period of time. It is often linked to lifestyle factors. To make it easier for people to determine if they have diabetes, we have created a system that allows them to enter their data and receive a prediction about whether they have diabetes or not. This system is not meant to replace doctors, but rather to provide them with a better understanding of the problem.
