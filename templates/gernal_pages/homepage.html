Problem Statements

Calories Burnt Prediction
Calculating the number of calories burned during exercise is an important part of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. While modern devices can help with this, they may not be accessible to everyone. This model allows anyone to easily predict their calorie burn during a specified time period, helping them track their progress and improve their fitness.
Diabetese Prediction
Diabetes is a metabolic disorder characterized by high blood sugar levels over a prolonged period of time. It is often linked to lifestyle factors. To make it easier for people to determine if they have diabetes, we have created a system that allows them to enter their data and receive a prediction about whether they have diabetes or not. This system is not meant to replace doctors, but rather to provide them with a better understanding of the problem.
Laptop Price Prediction
It is easy to get tricked into paying too much for a laptop in an electronic market. This tool uses data on laptop specs to predict the price of a specific laptop, helping buyers avoid overpaying and shop owners prove their prices are fair.
